Getting There

🚗 By Car

From the North: Take Highway 101 South to Exit 15 (Downtown). Turn right on Main Street, then left on Wedding Avenue.

From the South: Take Highway 101 North to Exit 15 (Downtown). Turn left on Main Street, then left on Wedding Avenue.

Parking: Complimentary valet parking available. Self-parking lot adjacent to venue.

✈️ By Air

Nearest Airport: Downtown Regional Airport (DRA) - 15 minutes

Major Airport: Metropolitan International (MIA) - 45 minutes

Transportation: Taxi, Uber/Lyft, or rental car available from both airports.

🚊 Public Transit

Metro Line: Blue Line to Downtown Station (2 blocks from venue)

Bus Routes: Routes 15, 22, and 45 stop within 3 blocks

Taxi/Rideshare: Uber and Lyft readily available in the area

Things to Do

Make the most of your visit! Here are some local attractions and activities to explore:

🎨 Downtown Art Museum

World-class art collection featuring contemporary and classical works. Open daily 10am-6pm.

🌳 Riverside Park

Beautiful waterfront park perfect for morning walks or picnics. Features walking trails and scenic views.

🍷 Wine District

Historic district with wine bars, tasting rooms, and boutique shops. Just 10 minutes from the venue.

🏛️ Historic Downtown

Charming historic district with unique shops, cafes, and restaurants. Perfect for afternoon strolling.

🎭 Theater District

Catch a show at one of the many theaters featuring Broadway productions and local performances.

🍴 Foodie Scene

Award-winning restaurants ranging from casual cafes to fine dining. Don't miss the famous farmers market!
